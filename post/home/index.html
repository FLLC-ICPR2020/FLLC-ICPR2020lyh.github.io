  • The 2nd grand challenge of 106-point facial landmark localization will be held in conjunction with the International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR) 2020, Milan, Italy.

Overview

Facial landmark localization is a very crucial step in numerous face related applications, such as face alignment for recognition, facial pose estimation, face image synthesis, etc. We are hosting the 2nd 106-point facial landmark localization competition in conjunction with ICPR 2020. The purpose is to make effort towards benchmarking lightweight facial landmark localization, which enable efficient system deployment. Different to the 1st competition, this year, we release more than 20,000 images with manual annotation of 106-point facial landmark, and employ a strict limit of model weights for computational efficiency. We sincerely invite academia and industrial practitioners to participate and together push the frontier along this direction.
